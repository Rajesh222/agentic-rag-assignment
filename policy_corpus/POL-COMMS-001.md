# External Communications Policy

_Document ID: POL-COMMS-001_

## Media Inquiries
All media inquiries must be routed to Marketing & Communications. Employees may not speak to the press on behalf of Meridian without prior approval.

## Social Media
Employees may post about Meridian on personal social media provided posts are consistent with the Code of Conduct. Do not share confidential or client information.

## Public Speaking
Speaking engagements in a professional capacity require approval from the line manager and Marketing.

## LinkedIn
Employees are encouraged to maintain a LinkedIn presence reflecting their Meridian role accurately. Do not list client engagements without client permission.

## Company Logo
The Meridian logo may not be used in personal contexts or in non-Meridian commercial contexts.
