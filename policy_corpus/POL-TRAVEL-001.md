# Business Travel Policy

_Document ID: POL-TRAVEL-001_

## Scope
This policy applies to all business travel undertaken on behalf of Meridian Consulting, both domestic within UAE and international.

## Pre-Approval
All business travel must be pre-approved via the Travel Request Form, submitted to the line manager at least **7 working days** before travel.

## Class of Travel
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class for flights over 6 hours AND for all travel by Directors and above

## Per Diem Rates
Per diem rates are defined in POL-TRAVEL-002 (Per Diem Schedule).

## Expense Reporting
Business travel expenses must be reported within 10 working days of return. See POL-FIN-003 (Expense Reimbursement) for the submission process.

## Safety
Travelers must register with the Travel Safety Platform before departure. For high-risk destinations (list maintained by Operations), additional security briefing is required.
