# Wellness Leave and Sick Day Practices

_Document ID: POL-HR-011_

## Wellness Philosophy
Meridian encourages employees to rest when unwell, without fear of losing leave balance.

## Sick Day Accumulation
**Unused sick days carry forward up to 10 days into the following year**, encouraging employees to take sick leave when genuinely needed rather than hoarding the balance.

## Wellness Checks
Quarterly wellness conversations with the line manager help identify stress and workload issues early.

## Absence Without Medical Certificate
Up to 2 consecutive sick days may be taken without a medical certificate.
