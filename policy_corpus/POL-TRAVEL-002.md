# Per Diem Schedule

_Document ID: POL-TRAVEL-002_

Per diem rates by destination category, effective 1 January 2025:

| Destination | Per Diem (AED/day) |
|---|---|
| Within UAE (outside home emirate) | 250 |
| GCC countries | 450 |
| Europe and North America | 750 |
| Africa and Asia (excluding Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong) | 500 |
| Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong | 750 |

Per diem covers meals and incidental expenses. Hotel is reimbursed separately up to the caps in POL-FIN-003.

## Within-UAE Travel for Client Meetings
For client meetings within the traveler's home emirate, no per diem applies. Meal expenses during client meetings are reimbursable up to AED 150 per meal, up to 2 meals per day.
