# Workplace Health and Safety

_Document ID: POL-OPS-003_

## General Obligations
All employees must take reasonable care of their own health and safety and that of others.

## Hazard Reporting
Workplace hazards must be reported to Facilities within 24 hours.

## First Aid
First-aid kits are maintained at reception and in each kitchen. Trained first-aiders are identified on the intranet.

## Fire Safety
Fire drills are conducted quarterly. Know your nearest emergency exit.

## Ergonomics
Workstation ergonomic assessments are available on request. Book via the Facilities Portal.
