# Data Protection and Privacy Policy

_Document ID: POL-IT-004_

## Scope
This policy applies to personal data processed by Meridian in the course of business.

## Lawful Basis
Meridian processes personal data under one or more of the following lawful bases: consent, contract performance, legal obligation, legitimate interest.

## Data Subject Rights
Individuals may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of their personal data. Requests are fulfilled within 30 calendar days.

## Data Retention
| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Employee records (active) | Duration of employment |
| Employee records (post-termination) | 7 years |
| Client engagement records | 7 years after engagement close |
| Marketing data | 3 years from last interaction |
| Financial transaction records | 10 years |

## Breach Notification
Personal data breaches must be reported to the DPO within 24 hours of discovery. Regulatory notification follows applicable law.
