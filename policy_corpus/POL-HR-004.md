# Remote Work Policy

_Document ID: POL-HR-004_

## Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probation period are eligible to work remotely for **up to 2 days per week**, subject to line manager approval.

## Core Hours
Remote workers must be available for synchronous collaboration between 10:00 and 16:00 Gulf Standard Time.

## Equipment
Meridian provides a laptop and a monitor. Employees are responsible for their home internet connection.

## Security Requirements
See POL-IT-002 (Remote Access Security). VPN usage is mandatory for all remote work.

## Remote-First Roles
Some roles are designated remote-first and are exempt from the 2-day cap. These are listed in the Remote-First Role Register maintained by HR.
