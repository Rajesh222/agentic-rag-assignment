# Intellectual Property Policy

_Document ID: POL-LEGAL-002_

## Work for Hire
All intellectual property created by employees in the course of their employment is owned by Meridian Consulting.

## Client IP
Unless explicitly agreed otherwise, IP created for clients is owned by the client upon payment.

## Third-Party IP
Use of third-party IP (open source, licensed content, stock imagery) must comply with applicable license terms. Maintain a record of all third-party components used in deliverables.

## Moonlighting
Employees who create IP outside working hours unrelated to Meridian business retain ownership of such IP, provided it does not compete with or use Meridian resources. Pre-clearance required via HR.

## Publication
Any publication or public presentation of Meridian work requires approval from the Marketing team and (for client-related content) the client.
