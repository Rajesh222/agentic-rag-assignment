# Leave Policy — General Overview

_Document ID: POL-HR-001_

Meridian Consulting provides the following leave entitlements to full-time employees:

## Annual Leave
Full-time employees are entitled to **22 working days of annual leave** per calendar year, accrued at a rate of 1.83 days per month of service completed. Annual leave cannot be carried forward beyond 5 days into the next calendar year without written approval from the line manager.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to **10 days of paid sick leave** per calendar year. A medical certificate is required for any absence longer than 2 consecutive working days. Sick leave does not accumulate across years.

## Parental Leave
Parental leave entitlements are defined in POL-HR-002 (Parental Leave Policy). This policy does not cover parental leave — refer to that document.

## Bereavement Leave
Employees are entitled to 5 working days of paid bereavement leave upon the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling). For extended family, 2 working days are granted.

## Public Holidays
Meridian observes all UAE federal public holidays as gazetted annually.

## Approval Process
All leave requests must be submitted via the HR Portal at least 10 working days in advance (except for sick leave). Emergency leave may be approved retrospectively by the line manager.
