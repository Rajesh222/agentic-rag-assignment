# Termination and Notice Period

_Document ID: POL-HR-007_

## Notice Period
**Standard notice period for resignation or termination is 60 calendar days** for all employees regardless of grade.

## Gardening Leave
Meridian reserves the right to place employees on gardening leave during the notice period. Employees on gardening leave continue to receive full compensation and benefits.

## Final Settlement
Final settlement including unused annual leave encashment is paid within 14 working days of the last working day.

## Non-Compete
Employees may not join a direct competitor for 6 months after termination. Direct competitors are defined in Schedule A.

## End of Service Gratuity
Calculated per UAE labor law.
