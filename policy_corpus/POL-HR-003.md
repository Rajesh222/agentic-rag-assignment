# Employee Handbook — Benefits Summary

_Document ID: POL-HR-003_

This handbook summarizes benefits available to Meridian employees. For authoritative detail, refer to the individual policy documents.

## Leave Benefits at a Glance
- Annual leave: 22 working days per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- **Paternity leave: 7 working days** (taken within 6 months of birth)
- Maternity leave: 60 calendar days paid
- Bereavement leave: up to 5 working days

## Insurance
Meridian provides comprehensive medical insurance for employees and up to 3 dependents.

## End of Service
End-of-service gratuity is paid according to UAE labor law.

## Training Budget
Each employee is entitled to an annual training budget of AED 5,000 which can be spent on courses, conferences, or certifications, subject to line manager approval.

> Note: This handbook is a summary. Where policies conflict between this handbook and standalone policy documents, the standalone policy documents are authoritative.
