# Client Gifts and Entertainment

_Document ID: POL-CLIENT-002_

## Gifts
Gifts to clients may not exceed **AED 500 per client per calendar year** without approval from the COO.

## Entertainment
Client entertainment (meals, events) is permitted within reasonable limits. Pre-approval required for events with a value over AED 2,000.

## Receiving Gifts
Gifts received from clients or vendors with a value over AED 300 must be declared to HR and may be required to be returned or donated.

## Government Clients
No gifts or entertainment may be offered to employees of government clients without legal review and COO approval.

## Documentation
All gifts given or received must be logged in the Gift Register maintained by Compliance.
