# Engagement Escalation Protocol

_Document ID: POL-CLIENT-003_

## Escalation Triggers
Escalate to Engagement Director when:
- Client relationship is at risk
- Deliverable is more than 5 working days late
- Scope dispute arises
- Quality issue is raised by client
- Payment is more than 30 days overdue

## Escalation Path
1. Engagement Manager addresses first
2. Engagement Director within 2 working days
3. COO if unresolved after 5 working days
4. CEO for strategic accounts

## Documentation
All escalations must be logged in the Engagement Risk Register within 24 hours.
