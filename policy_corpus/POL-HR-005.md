# Code of Conduct

_Document ID: POL-HR-005_

All Meridian employees are expected to uphold the following standards of conduct.

## Integrity
Act with honesty in all dealings with clients, colleagues, and vendors. Do not misrepresent facts, credentials, or deliverables.

## Conflicts of Interest
Declare any potential conflict of interest in writing to HR within 5 working days of becoming aware of it. This includes personal relationships with vendors, clients, or competitors.

## Confidentiality
All client information is confidential. Do not share client data with third parties without explicit written authorization.

## Anti-Discrimination
Meridian is an equal-opportunity employer. Discrimination or harassment on any protected basis will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

## Reporting Misconduct
Report suspected misconduct to HR or via the anonymous Ethics Hotline.
