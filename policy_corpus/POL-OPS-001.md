# Meeting Etiquette and Standards

_Document ID: POL-OPS-001_

## Agenda
All meetings over 30 minutes require a circulated agenda.

## Punctuality
Meetings start on time. Attendees arriving more than 5 minutes late should join silently and catch up from the notes.

## Minutes
Meetings with decisions or action items require written minutes circulated within 1 working day.

## Meeting-Free Time
Wednesday afternoons (14:00-17:00) are designated meeting-free for focus work. Exceptions require department head approval.

## Virtual Meeting Standards
Cameras on by default for internal meetings of 6 people or fewer. Mute when not speaking.
