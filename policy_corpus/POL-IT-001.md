# Acceptable Use Policy

_Document ID: POL-IT-001_

## Company Devices
Company-issued devices must be used primarily for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with work duties or company security.

## Prohibited Activities
- Installing unauthorized software
- Accessing illegal content
- Using company resources for personal commercial gain
- Sharing login credentials

## Monitoring
Meridian reserves the right to monitor use of company systems and devices. Employees have no expectation of privacy when using company resources.

## BYOD
Personal devices may be used to access email and calendar only, and must be enrolled in the mobile device management system.
