# Expense Reimbursement Policy

_Document ID: POL-FIN-003_

## Submission Window
**Expense reimbursement claims must be submitted within 15 working days** of the expense being incurred. Claims submitted after this window require department head approval and may be rejected.

## Required Documentation
- Original receipt (digital scan accepted)
- Project code or cost center
- Business purpose
- Attendees (for entertainment expenses)

## Hotel Caps by Destination
| Destination | Hotel Cap (AED/night) |
|---|---|
| Within UAE | 800 |
| GCC | 1,200 |
| Europe and North America | 2,000 |
| Rest of world | 1,500 |

## Non-Reimbursable
- Alcohol (except client entertainment with explicit pre-approval)
- Fines, penalties, parking tickets
- Personal items

## Foreign Currency
Expenses in foreign currency are reimbursed at the mid-market rate on the date of expense.
