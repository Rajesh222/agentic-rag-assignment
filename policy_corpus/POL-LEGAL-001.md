# Contract Approval Authority

_Document ID: POL-LEGAL-001_

## Approval Matrix

| Contract Value (AED) | Approver |
|---|---|
| Under 50,000 | Department Head |
| 50,000–250,000 | COO |
| 250,000–1,000,000 | CEO |
| Over 1,000,000 | Board |

## Legal Review
All contracts over AED 50,000 require Legal review before signing. Standard NDAs and MSAs from pre-approved templates do not require additional Legal review when used unmodified.

## Signing Authority
Only officers with documented signing authority may execute contracts. Signing authority is maintained in the Authority Matrix on the Legal SharePoint.

## Contract Repository
Executed contracts must be uploaded to the Contract Repository within 5 working days of signing.
