# Procurement Policy

_Document ID: POL-FIN-001_

## Thresholds
All purchases must follow the appropriate approval threshold:
- Under AED 5,000: line manager approval
- AED 5,000–50,000: department head approval, 3 quotes required
- AED 50,000–500,000: CFO approval, formal RFP required
- Over AED 500,000: Board approval required

## Approved Vendor List
Use vendors from the Approved Vendor List maintained by Procurement. New vendors require onboarding per POL-FIN-004.

## Emergency Purchases
Emergency purchases may bypass the quote requirement with documented justification. Post-facto approval must be obtained within 5 working days.

## Contracts
All vendor contracts over AED 50,000 require Legal review.
