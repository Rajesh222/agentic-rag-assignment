# Business Continuity

_Document ID: POL-OPS-002_

## Scope
Meridian maintains a business continuity capability to ensure service delivery during disruptions.

## Critical Functions
The following are designated as critical functions:
- Client service delivery
- Payroll
- IT infrastructure

## Recovery Objectives
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours for critical functions
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 1 hour for client data

## Testing
BCM plans are tested annually. Test results are reported to the Board.

## Incident Activation
BCM is activated by the COO or delegate. Activation triggers are defined in the BCM Plan.
