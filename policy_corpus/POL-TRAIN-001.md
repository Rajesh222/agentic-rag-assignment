# Training and Development Policy

_Document ID: POL-TRAIN-001_

## Annual Budget
Each full-time employee receives an annual training budget of **AED 6,000**, available for professional development courses, certifications, and conferences.

## Approval
Training expenses require line manager approval. Certifications from approved bodies are pre-authorized.

## Time Off
Up to 5 working days per year may be taken for training events with no impact on annual leave.

## Commitment Period
Training above AED 10,000 (when approved as an exception) requires a 12-month retention commitment. Early leavers reimburse pro-rata.

## Professional Memberships
Professional memberships relevant to the employee's role are reimbursable up to AED 2,500 per year (separate from training budget).
