# Performance Review Policy

_Document ID: POL-HR-006_

Performance reviews are conducted twice yearly, in June and December.

## Process
1. Self-assessment by the employee
2. Peer feedback (optional, by employee nomination)
3. Manager assessment
4. Calibration meeting at department level
5. Final rating communicated within 2 weeks of the review cycle

## Rating Scale
- **5 — Exceptional**: Exceeds all objectives
- **4 — Strong**: Exceeds most objectives
- **3 — Solid**: Meets all objectives
- **2 — Developing**: Meets some objectives, requires support
- **1 — Unsatisfactory**: Does not meet objectives

## Performance Improvement Plans
Employees rated 1 or 2 will be placed on a 90-day Performance Improvement Plan.

## Compensation Review
Annual compensation reviews are conducted each January. Mid-year ratings inform the following January review.
