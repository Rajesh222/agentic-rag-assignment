# Parental Leave Policy

_Document ID: POL-HR-002_

Meridian Consulting is committed to supporting employees through major family milestones.

## Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to **60 calendar days of paid maternity leave** following the birth of a child. An additional 15 days of unpaid leave may be taken with manager approval.

## Paternity Leave
Male employees are entitled to **5 working days of paid paternity leave**, to be taken within the first 3 months after the birth of the child.

## Adoption Leave
Employees adopting a child below the age of 5 are entitled to the same benefits as biological parents.

## Return to Work
Employees returning from parental leave are entitled to request flexible working arrangements for the first 6 months, subject to business needs.

## Relationship to Other Policies
This policy supersedes any parental-leave provisions in the general Leave Policy (POL-HR-001). In case of conflict between this document and POL-HR-001 regarding parental matters, this policy takes precedence.
