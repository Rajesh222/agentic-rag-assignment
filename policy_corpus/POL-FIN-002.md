# Corporate Card Usage

_Document ID: POL-FIN-002_

Meridian issues corporate credit cards to employees at Manager grade and above.

## Approved Uses
- Business travel expenses (airfare, hotel, ground transportation)
- Client entertainment within authorized limits
- Emergency business supplies

## Prohibited Uses
- Personal expenses of any kind
- Cash advances
- Unapproved vendor payments

## Reconciliation
Monthly corporate card statements must be reconciled and submitted with receipts by the 10th of the following month.

## Lost Cards
Report lost or stolen cards to Finance and the card issuer immediately.
